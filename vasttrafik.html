User research

To really get to know our users, we dove into some serious research. We started by creating a user persona, basically a detailed profile of a typical app user.
We did this through surveys and chats, which helped us really understand what our users needed and what bugged them.

Process

Our research pointed out three big problems that needed fixing, based on the data and feedback we got.
To come up with ideas to solve these problems, we started with the "Crazy 8" technique. We brainstormed and had workshops where we shared ideas. We ended up picking three solutions that struck a balance between being effective and simple.

Prototype

We put our chosen designs to the test with real users to make sure they were easy to use and did what they were supposed to.
